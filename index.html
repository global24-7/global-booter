FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I deposit with MoMo?▼
Click "Add Funds" in your dashboard. You'll be redirected to our secure Paystack payment page. Select Mobile Money, enter your MoMo number and amount, then confirm the prompt on your phone. Your balance is credited instantly after confirmation.
How fast will I receive my order?▼
Most orders begin within 1–10 minutes. Delivery speed depends on the service and quantity. Larger orders may take slightly longer but are always completed within the estimated time shown during checkout.
Is my account safe?▼
Yes! We never ask for your account password. We only need your public profile link or username. Our methods are safe and comply with platform guidelines. However, ensure your account is public before placing an order.
What if my followers drop?▼
We offer a 30-day refill guarantee on most services. If your followers drop within 30 days of delivery, contact us and we'll refill them for free. Note: drops may occur due to platform purges — this is outside our control.
How does the referral program work?▼
Share your unique referral link with friends. When they make their first purchase, you earn 3 GHS and they get 2 GHS credited to their account. You can refer up to 10 friends, earning up to 30 GHS total.
What is a Claim Code?▼
Claim codes are special codes generated by our admin that add funds to your account instantly. You may receive these through promotions, giveaways, or special offers. Enter the code in your dashboard to redeem.
Do you offer refunds?▼
We offer refunds if an order cannot be delivered. If delivery fails, funds are returned to your account balance automatically. Cash refunds are processed within 3–5 business days upon request. See our Refund Policy for full details.
